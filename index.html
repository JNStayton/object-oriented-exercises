Welcome to THE GARAGE

  • In the console, create a vehicle: let myVehicle = new Vehicle(make, model, year)

  • In the console, create your car: let myCar = new Car(make, model, year)

  • In the console, create a motorcycle: let myRide = new Motorcycle(make, model, year)

  • Cars have a .honk() method, while motorcycles have a .revEngine() method.

  • In the console, add your garage: let myGarage = new Garage(vehicleCapacity);

  • Use the garage method .add(vehicle) to fill your garage! Only vehicles accepted.
